Sargam colors are the language of the beauty, the warmth, and the caress, that comes from the heart of the world. The most direct expression of beauty, with a form and spirit which is one and simple, and least encumbered with anything extraneous.

A color system Tokens & translation workflow of emancipation which makes it easy to recognize the beauty in small things. True designers, they who are seers, seek to express the universe with sargam colors.

Installation

Install Sargam Colors from your terminal via npm. Don't be afraid to make these big decisions. Once you start, they sort of just make themselves. Nothing's gonna make your husband or wife madder than coming home and having a snow-covered dinner. Let your heart take you to wherever you want to be. If you don't like it - change it. It's your world. That easy.

                npm i @sargamdesign/colors

Import vanilla CSS and few more words comes here to make it multiple lines text. I really believe that if you practice enough you could paint the 'Mona Lisa' with a two-inch brush.

                import "@sargamdesign/colors/dist/themes/light.css";
                import "@sargamdesign/colors/dist/themes/dark.css";

Use the CDN files and few more words comes here to make it multiple lines text. If you hypnotize it, it will go away. You create the dream - then you bring it into your world. Let's have a happy little tree in here. Brown is such a nice color. You have to allow the paint to break to make it beautiful.

                <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/@sargamdesign/colors@1.2.1/dist/themes/light.min.css">
                <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/npm/@sargamdesign/colors@1.2.1/dist/themes/dark.min.css">

[Note] Using the CDN approach in production is not recommended. It's intended for prototyping only.
